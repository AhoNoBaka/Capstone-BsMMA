What is BMMA?

Bachelor of Multimedia Arts is a four-year undergraduate course that combines art with modern technology and teaches skills and knowledge that is often used in producing content in modern media platforms. This program equips students with essential skills in graphic design, animation, video production, photography, and interactive media, preparing them for careers in advertising, film, and digital marketing, and other multimedia-related firms.

The Curriculum

1st Year

Drawing 1 and 2, Anatomy, Perspective, Values

2nd Year

2D Animation, 3D Modelling, and 3D Animation
Animation techniques, in-betweening, 3D modelling and animation on Blender

3rd Year

Photography and Film
Composition, Proper use of Aperture, Iso and Shutter Speed, Color Grading

4th Year

Audio and Video Editing
Foley, Editing Dialogue, Mixing, and Mastering
